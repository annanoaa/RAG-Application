# Sample Questions for Testing the RAG Application

## Questions for Climate Change Document:

1. What is the main driver of climate change since the 1800s?
2. How much has the Earth's average surface temperature risen?
3. What are the two main approaches to address climate change?
4. When was the Paris Agreement signed?

## Questions for Quarterly Report Document:

1. What was TechnoVate's revenue in Q1 2025?
2. Which division showed the highest growth rate?
3. Where did TechnoVate open new offices in Q1 2025?
4. Who is the CEO of TechnoVate?
