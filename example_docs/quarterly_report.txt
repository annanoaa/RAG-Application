TECHNOVATE SOLUTIONS, INC.
QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT
Q1 2025

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

TechnoVate Solutions, Inc. ("TechnoVate" or "the Company") is pleased to present its financial results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2025. Despite challenging market conditions, TechnoVate has demonstrated resilience and strategic growth in key business areas.

Key Highlights:
• Revenue increased by 18.5% to $78.3 million compared to Q1 2024
• Operating profit margin improved to 23.4%, up from 19.8% in Q1 2024
• Cloud services division grew by 42.3% year-over-year
• Successfully launched TechnoVate AI Assistant, exceeding initial adoption targets by 35%
• Expanded international presence with new offices in Singapore and Berlin

FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE

Revenue
Total revenue for Q1 2025 was $78.3 million, representing an 18.5% increase from $66.1 million in Q1 2024. This growth was primarily driven by:

• Cloud Services: $34.2 million (43.7% of total revenue)
• Enterprise Solutions: $21.5 million (27.4% of total revenue)
• AI Products: $15.8 million (20.2% of total revenue)
• Consulting Services: $6.8 million (8.7% of total revenue)

The Cloud Services division continued its strong performance with 42.3% year-over-year growth, reflecting increased customer demand for secure, scalable cloud infrastructure. Our AI Products division showed the highest growth rate at 63.5% year-over-year, largely due to the successful launch of TechnoVate AI Assistant in January 2025.

Expenses
Operating expenses totaled $59.9 million in Q1 2025, compared to $53.0 million in Q1 2024. The increase was primarily due to:

• Research and Development: $18.3 million (30.6% of operating expenses)
• Sales and Marketing: $16.7 million (27.9% of operating expenses)
• General and Administrative: $14.8 million (24.7% of operating expenses)
• Cost of Services: $10.1 million (16.8% of operating expenses)

R&D expenses increased by 24.5% compared to Q1 2024, reflecting our continued investment in AI technologies and cloud infrastructure improvements. Despite the overall increase in expenses, improved operational efficiency resulted in operating expenses declining as a percentage of revenue from 80.2% in Q1 2024 to 76.6% in Q1 2025.

Profitability
Operating income for Q1 2025 was $18.4 million, representing a 59.3% increase from $11.5 million in Q1 2024. The operating profit margin improved to 23.4%, compared to 19.8% in the same period last year. Net income reached $14.2 million, or $1.28 per diluted share, compared to $8.9 million, or $0.82 per diluted share, in Q1 2024.

BUSINESS HIGHLIGHTS

Product Development
TechnoVate launched several key products and enhancements during Q1 2025:

• TechnoVate AI Assistant, our flagship conversational AI platform, was launched in January 2025 and has already been adopted by over 1,200 enterprise customers.
• CloudSecure 5.0 was released with enhanced encryption and compliance features for regulated industries.
• EnterprisePro Suite received significant updates to improve integration capabilities with third-party applications.

The Company invested $18.3 million in R&D during the quarter, representing 23.4% of total revenue, compared to 22.3% in Q1 2024.

Market Expansion
International expansion continued to be a strategic focus in Q1 2025:

• New offices were established in Singapore and Berlin to better serve clients in Asia-Pacific and European markets.
• International revenue grew to 38.5% of total revenue, compared to 32.7% in Q1 2024.
• The Company secured major contracts with government agencies in Australia and Canada.

The Company plans to continue its geographic expansion with a focus on emerging markets in Southeast Asia and Latin America in the coming quarters.

OUTLOOK FOR 2025

Based on current business momentum and market conditions, TechnoVate is providing the following guidance for fiscal year 2025:

• Revenue is expected to be between $330 million and $345 million, representing 16-21% growth over fiscal year 2024.
• Operating profit margin is expected to be in the range of 24-26%.
• Diluted earnings per share are expected to be between $5.35 and $5.65.

The Company remains cautiously optimistic about its growth prospects given ongoing macroeconomic uncertainties. Management is closely monitoring inflation, interest rates, and global supply chain challenges that could impact business performance.

TechnoVate is committed to investing in innovation while maintaining financial discipline. The Company plans to allocate approximately 22-24% of its revenue to R&D initiatives, with a particular focus on advancing its AI capabilities and enhancing its cloud infrastructure.

CONCLUSION

TechnoVate Solutions, Inc. delivered strong financial and operational results in Q1 2025, demonstrating the effectiveness of its growth strategy and the value of its innovative product offerings. The Company remains well-positioned to capitalize on the growing demand for cloud services and AI solutions.
